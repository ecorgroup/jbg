Jeff Beasley

Endorses:


Jeff Beasley holds degrees in Music and Classical Guitar, is a prolific educator, popular workshop and clinic instructor, and former columnist for Premier Guitar magazine. Jeff has worked with some of the biggest names in guitar, and is a senior faculty member with Truefire. Jeff has appeared in Guitarist Network magazine, Guitar For The Practicng Musician, Guitar Player, Music For The World, Music and Arts, Musicians Hotline, and Premier Guitar magazines. Jeff has 35 years' experience in studio, teaching and performance. Jeff is endorsed by Dean Guitars, Sierra Guitars, D'Addario Strings, In Tune Guitar Picks, ProCo Cables and Keeley Effects.
